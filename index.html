Welcome Message

From the Parish Priest Desk

"To find God in all things, and to walk together under the banner of Christ’s love."

Welcome to our vibrant digital parish environment. Here at St. Francis, administered under the spiritual stewardship of the Society of Jesus (Jesuits), we dedicate ourselves to intellectual growth, deep spiritual formation, and robust social outreach metrics. Whether you are a lifelong parishioner or searching for a spiritual home, you are welcome here.

Our History

Our Sacred Journey

Milestones of faith, community resilience, and Jesuit dedication over the years.

1999

The Foundation Stones

First Holy Mass celebrated on the layout grounds with our pioneering Jesuit community leaders and a small group of local families.

2012

Sanctuary Dedication

Completion and consecration of our primary church building to accommodate over 3,000 worshippers securely.

2026

A Modern Archdiocesan Pillar

Expanding our community structures, digital tracking access, and launching comprehensive parish tracking tools.

The Holy Sacraments

Sacred rituals instituted by Christ that nurture, heal, and sustain our spiritual walk.

Holy Baptism

The gateway to life in the Spirit and the door to all other sacraments.

Requirements:

Parents and Godparents must complete the parish preparatory catechesis tracking class.

Register / Inquire

First Holy Communion

Receiving the true Body, Blood, Soul, and Divinity of Jesus Christ in the Eucharist.

Requirements:

Continuous enrollment in our Saturday children's or adult RCIA catechesis program.

Register / Inquire

Confirmation

Sealing the baptized with the gift of the Holy Spirit to strengthen their Christian witness.

Requirements:

Available for teenagers and adults who have completed intermediate sacramental instruction.

Register / Inquire

Holy Matrimony

A sacred covenant reflecting the love and unity between Christ and His Church.

Requirements:

Couples must notify the parish office at least 6 months prior to setting their date.

Reconciliation

The healing sacrament of confession where we receive God's infinite mercy and forgiveness.

Schedule:

Saturdays after early Morning Mass and by appointment with our parish priests.

Parish Ministries & Associations

Discover your calling and serve the community through one of our diverse active lay groups.

Altar Servers Association

Assisting the clergy reverently at the altar during holy liturgy settings.

Meetings: Saturdays 4:00 PM

The Parish Choir

Leading the congregation into deeper liturgical prayer through traditional and contemporary sacred music.

Rehearsals: Tuesdays & Thursdays 6:30 PM

Catholic Youth Organization (CYON)

Uniting young adults through faith-sharing, leadership training, and social impact projects.

Meetings: 2nd Sundays after Youth Mass

Legion of Mary

Apostolic service, spiritual tracking, and home visitation under our Lady's patronage.

Meetings: Sundays 4:30 PM

Catholic Men Organization (CMO)

Guiding fathers to build solid Christian homes and provide leadership for parish development.

Meetings: 1st Sundays after Early Mass

Catholic Women Organization (CWO)

Fostering prayerful fellowship and maternal support systems among our mothers.

Meetings: 2nd Sundays after Midday Mass
